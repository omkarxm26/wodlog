HEALTH & MUSCLE PLAN
Personalised for 6-day/week CrossFit athletes
Your Profile
Log body metrics in Daily Log to see your profile.
12-Week Phases
Weeks 1–4 · Strength Accumulation
Build a strength base with compound lifts. Keep CrossFit intensity moderate. 2g protein/kg/day minimum.
Weeks 5–8 · Hypertrophy Block
Volume-focused. 3–4 sets of 8–12 reps on accessory work. Caloric surplus of 200–300 kcal.
Weeks 9–12 · Performance Peak
Intensity up, volume down. Prioritize WOD performance and 1RM testing. Maintain protein.
Key Principles
🥩
Protein First
2–2.4g/kg daily. Spread across 4–5 meals. Leucine-rich sources to maximise MPS.
😴
Sleep = Gains
7–9 hours mandatory. GH peaks in deep sleep. One bad night cuts testosterone by 15%.
📈
Progressive Overload
Track PRs. Add 2–5% load every 1–2 weeks. What doesn't get tracked doesn't improve.
Macro Targets
Log body weight in Daily Log to see personalised targets.
Meal Timing
Pre-WOD (60–90 min before)
30–40g carbs + 20g protein. Oats + protein shake, rice + chicken. Avoid high fat/fibre.
Post-WOD (within 45 min)
40–60g carbs + 30–40g protein immediately. Anabolic window — don't skip it.
Before Sleep
Casein protein 30–40g (cottage cheese, Greek yoghurt, casein shake). Sustains MPS overnight.
Supplement Stack
Creatine Monohydrate
5g/day. Most evidence-backed supplement for strength + power. Timing flexible.
Caffeine
3–6mg/kg BW, 30–60 min pre-WOD. Improves power output and pain tolerance.
Omega-3 Fish Oil
2–3g EPA+DHA daily. Reduces inflammation, supports joints. Take with meals.
Vitamin D3 + K2
2000–5000 IU D3 + 100mcg K2. Testosterone, bone density, immune function.
Magnesium Glycinate
300–400mg before bed. Improves sleep quality and muscle relaxation.
Electrolytes
Na, K, Mg. Crucial for 6-day athletes. Himalayan salt in water pre-WOD.
Recovery Protocol
Sleep Optimisation
Fixed wake time. Room at 18–20°C. No screens 60 min before bed. Magnesium 30 min before sleep. 7–9 hours — GH is released and muscles repair here.
Active Recovery (Sunday)
20–30 min walk, swim, or yoga. Light movement flushes lactate. Mobility work 15–20 min: hips, shoulders, thoracic spine.
Cold & Heat
Cold shower 2–3 min post-WOD reduces inflammation. Sauna 2–3×/week (15–20 min at 80°C) boosts GH by 200–300%.
Deload Week (Every 4–6 Weeks)
Cut volume 40–50%, keep intensity. Super-compensation happens here. Signs you need one: persistent soreness, poor sleep, motivation drops, WOD regression.
Weekly Training Template — 6-Day Split
Monday — Lower Body Strength
Back Squat 5×5 or Front Squat 4×4 + Deadlift variation. WOD with lower-body bias: thrusters, box jumps, lunges.
Tuesday — Upper Body Push + Pull
Bench/Press + Pull-up strength work. Gymnastics skill (HSPU, muscle-ups). WOD: push/pull movements, 3–4 sets accessory.
Wednesday — Olympic Lifting
Snatch or Clean & Jerk technique + heavy singles. Highest skill day — do when fresh. WOD: lighter aerobic bias conditioning.
Thursday — Aerobic Capacity
Long AMRAP (20–30 min) or For Time with row/run/bike. Focus on pacing, breathing, aerobic engine development.
Friday — Mixed Modal / Test Day
Benchmark WOD (Fran, Helen, Diane) or PR attempt. Fresh from Wednesday. Track all scores — this is your progress marker.
Saturday — Community / Partner WOD
High-carb day to replenish glycogen. Enjoy the class. Moderate intensity — full week behind you.
Sunday — Full Rest
Meal prep. Sleep in. Hydrate. Recovery is part of training.
